# A/R Automation Instruction Sheet

Last updated: May 4, 2026

## Links

- Upload the prepared aging A/R workbook here: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1X1RbqyF_nzCHk2ggLcnRuA3D8s5nWts3
- Submit the reviewed spreadsheet here: https://placek-jared-crm.app.n8n.cloud/form/ar-automation-v2-step-2-google-docs

## Steps

1. Open the aging A/R spreadsheet received from Accounting.

2. Remove the first tab from the workbook.

3. Save the updated workbook as an Excel file.

4. Upload the updated Excel file to the A/R upload folder:
   https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1X1RbqyF_nzCHk2ggLcnRuA3D8s5nWts3

5. Wait for the review spreadsheet email.

6. Open the review spreadsheet from the email.

7. Go to the `Review Projects` tab.

8. Review each row.

9. Leave `Send` as `Yes` for projects that should get a follow-up email.

10. Change `Send` away from `Yes` for projects that should not get a follow-up email.

11. Add notes in `Note For Distribution` if the sender needs special instructions.

12. Copy the review spreadsheet link.

13. Submit the review spreadsheet link here:
    https://placek-jared-crm.app.n8n.cloud/form/ar-automation-v2-step-2-google-docs

14. Wait for the Google Doc email.

15. Open the Google Doc from the email.

16. Review the prepared email text.

17. Make any needed edits before sending.

18. Attach the correct invoice PDFs manually.

19. Send the final emails manually.

## Important Notes

- Do not edit the `Parsed Invoices` or `Workflow Metadata` tabs.
- Do not send the Google Doc text without reviewing it first.
- The automation does not attach invoice PDFs.
- The automation does not send client emails automatically.
